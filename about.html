Developer, Designer & Learning Enthusiast

I'm a graduate from Georgia Tech with a MS in Computer Science. I specialized in Visual Analytics. At Tech, I was part of the GT VIS group. I worked as a Graduate Research Assistant under Dr. John Stasko for the most part, developing tools for exploring text and document collections. I also worked with Dr. Alex Endert on designing interaction techniques to improve time series prediction and Dr. Rahul Basole on health informatics. My interests include application development and visualization (visual analytics, information visualization, and digital design).

I am always looking to learn new things and improve myself. If you see someone breaking into a dance pose in the middle of nowhere, don't be scared, it is probably just me :)
