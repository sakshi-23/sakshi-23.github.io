Clinical Decision Support System

Project done in collaboration Collaborated with Professors from GSU and Doctors from Emory University as part of the Health Informatics course

Unplanned hospital readmissions are a big problem in health care, and not just for the patient. The goal of the project was to develop a Clinical Decision Support System for Hospital Discharge Decision Making
The steps already completed by the team were:
  • Extract a large sample of (de-identified) patient data from the data warehouse of Emory University Hospital
  • Develop a predictive model of 30-day readmission
  • Develop a first (alpha) version of a Clinical Decision Support System (CDSS) based on modelâ€™s predictions
  • Conduct laboratory experiments to test uptake of the alpha CDSS under alternative information and compensation treatments

My role in the project was to help them use visualization as a tool so as to make the system more trustable and efficient. The project was completed successfully and was able to help them with their requirements.
